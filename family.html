The Struggles of Penelope and Telemachus

While Odysseus makes his way home, Penelope tries to fend off over 100 suitors who seek her hand in marriage for a chance to rule the land of Ithaca. While invading Odysseus’ palace, the suitors indulge in large amounts of food and wine from the palace’s pantry. Penelope and Telemachus struggle to keep the suitors at bay and yearn for Odysseus’ return.
